Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25th January 2025

1. Introduction

Volunteer Connect ("we," "us," or "our") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information when you interact with our website, mobile applications, and other online services (collectively, the "Services").

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone, address
  • Demographic Information: Age, gender, interests, skills
  • Account Information: Username, hashed password
  • Volunteer Activity: Hours, assignments, preferences

Technical Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, site interaction

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from You: Account registration, contact forms
  • Automatically: Service usage tracking
  • Third Parties: Social media platform connections

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve Services
  • Facilitate volunteer matching
  • Account-related communications
  • Marketing (with consent)
  • Research and analysis
  • Legal compliance

5. Information Disclosure

  • Third-party service providers
  • Volunteer organizations
  • Legal authorities
  • Potential business transfers

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information, acknowledging that no internet transmission is completely secure.

7. Your Choices

  • Access and update personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability
  • Request account deletion

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their personal information.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically and will notify you of material changes.
